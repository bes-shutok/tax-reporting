## Terminology

- Official downloads: files mirrored locally from public official portals.
- Non-downloaded references: URLs tracked for periodic verification without local mirror.

## Official downloads

1. `official/at_folheto_criptoativos_2026-01-12.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/apoio_contribuinte/Folhetos_informativos/Documents/Criptoativos.pdf
- Issuing date: 2026-01-12
- Purpose: AT overview of crypto taxation categories and declaration guidance.
- Relevant provisions consulted: CIRS art. 10(17)-(22), art. 43(6)(g) and (7), art. 72, art. 124-A as summarized in the leaflet.

2. `official/at_oficio_circulado_20269_2024.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/legislacao/instrucoes_administrativas/Documents/Oficio_circulado_20269_2024.pdf
- Issuing date: 2024-03-24
- Purpose: Administrative instruction with detailed declaration treatment for crypto in IRS annexes.
- Relevant sections consulted: sections 8.6, 8.8, 9, and 12.6 for englobamento, Anexo G/G1 placement, and Anexo J Quadro 9.4 reporting.

3. `official/at_oficio_circulado_20278_2025.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/legislacao/instrucoes_administrativas/Documents/Oficio_circulado_20278_2025.pdf
- Issuing date: 2025-03-17
- Purpose: Updated Modelo 3 filing instructions referenced for 2025 tax-year filing.
- Relevant sections consulted: section 12.3.2 for the updated Quadro 9.4 structure used in 2025 filing guidance.

4. `official/dre_portaria_104_2026.pdf`
- URL: https://files.diariodarepublica.pt/1s/2026/03/04500/0007200236.pdf
- Issuing date: 2026-03-05
- Purpose: Official Portaria publication used as the origin file for the IRS declaration package update.
- Relevant provisions consulted: articles 1-4, especially the annex approval and in-force status for Modelo 3 and Anexos E, G, G1, and J.

5. `official/cirs_2025-07_code_consolidated.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/codigos_tributarios/Cod_download/Documents/CIRS_07_2025.pdf
- Issuing date: 2025-05-20 (AT PDF metadata; portal filename uses `07_2025`)
- Purpose: Consolidated CIRS source used to verify article 5 treatment of crypto remuneration.
- Relevant provisions consulted: CIRS art. 5(11), art. 10(17)-(22), art. 43(6)(g), (7), and (9), art. 44, art. 45, art. 48, art. 52, art. 72, art. 124-A.

6. `official/modelo3_anexo_j_2025.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/apoio_contribuinte/modelos_formularios/IRS/Documents/Mod_3_anexo_J.pdf
- Issuing date: 2026-02-19 (AT PDF metadata; form applies to 2025 tax-year filing)
- Purpose: Official Anexo J form and instructions used to verify Quadro 8A fields, Table V crypto codes, and Table X country codes.
- Relevant sections consulted: Tabela V (income codes), Tabela X (country codes), and the instructions for the foreign-source reporting fields referenced from Anexo G and G1.

7. `official/at_piv_22065_2023-11-06.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/informacoes_vinculativas/rendimento/cirs/Documents/PIV_22065.pdf
- Issuing date: 2023-11-06
- Purpose: Official AT binding ruling used to confirm that crypto-denominated remuneration is taxed on later disposal rather than at receipt.
- Relevant provisions consulted: CIRS art. 3(1), art. 5(2)(u) and (11), art. 10(1)(k) and (17)-(19), plus LOE 2023 art. 220 as discussed in the ruling.

8. `official/at_piv_21506_undated.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/informacoes_vinculativas/rendimento/cirs/Documents/PIV_21506.pdf
- Issuing date: undated PDF on portal
- Purpose: Official AT binding ruling used to confirm treatment of receipts in virtual currency.
- Relevant provisions consulted: CIRS art. 24 on in-kind income, art. 115 on invoicing obligations, art. 5(2)(u) and (11), art. 10(17)-(19), and LOE 2023 art. 220 as discussed in the ruling.

9. `official/modelo3_anexo_e_2025.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/apoio_contribuinte/modelos_formularios/IRS/Documents/Mod_3_anexo_E.pdf
- Issuing date: 2026-03-06 (PDF metadata mod date; form applies to 2025 tax-year filing)
- Purpose: Official Anexo E form and instructions used to confirm the immediate category E reporting code for non-crypto-form remuneration.
- Relevant sections consulted: Quadro 4A income-code table, especially code E21 for category E remuneration from crypto-asset operations that does not itself take the form of crypto-assets.

10. `official/modelo3_anexo_g_2026.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/apoio_contribuinte/modelos_formularios/IRS/Documents/Mod_3_anexo_G.pdf
- Issuing date: 2026-03-06 (portal Last-Modified header; form applies to 2025 tax-year filing)
- Purpose: Official Anexo G form and instructions used to verify Quadro 18 disposal reporting fields for crypto-asset gains.
- Relevant sections consulted: Quadro 18, Quadro 18A, and Quadro 18B, including the instructions for `Entidade Gestora`, `Despesas e encargos`, and `País da contraparte`.

11. `official/modelo3_anexo_g1_2025.pdf`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/apoio_contribuinte/modelos_formularios/IRS/Documents/Mod_3_anexo_G1.pdf
- Issuing date: 2025-03-03 (portal Last-Modified header; form applies to 2024 tax-year filing)
- Purpose: Official Anexo G1 form and instructions used to confirm the long-held asset reporting annex referenced alongside crypto disposal reporting.
- Relevant sections consulted: Quadro 7, including the instructions for long-held (`>=365 dias`) crypto disposals, `Entidade Gestora`, `Despesas e encargos`, and `País da contraparte`.

12. `official/cirs_art10_portal_2026-04-01.html`
- URL: https://info.portaldasfinancas.gov.pt/pt/informacao_fiscal/codigos_tributarios/cirs_rep/Pages/irs10.aspx
- Issuing date: undated portal page mirrored on 2026-04-01
- Purpose: Current AT portal rendering of CIRS article 10 archived to preserve the exact wording consulted for crypto disposal references.
- Relevant provisions consulted: CIRS art. 10(17)-(22), especially the crypto-asset definition, 365-day exclusion, crypto-to-crypto deferral, non-qualifying counterparty rule, and deemed disposal on exit from Portuguese residence.
